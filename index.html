About Me

My name’s Bagus Rizky Febriyanto but just call me Bagus (or Mas Gud, if you’re feeling fancy). Keep it chill. Welcome to my portfolio, built with curiosity, kopi, and way too many “one more tweak” kind of nights.

This space is basically my digital home—a place where I share my projects, growth, and a little bit of how I survive tech chaos. By day, I work as a Technical Support Engineer, aka the person people call when laptops freeze, Wi-Fi disappears, or printers suddenly develop attitude.

I like breaking things on purpose, fixing them, and figuring out how systems actually work. I’m always tinkering, always learning, and lowkey obsessed with making tech behave. Feel free to scroll around and enjoy the vibes.
